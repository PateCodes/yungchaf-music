# **App Name**: Yung Chaf Hub

## Core Features:

- Homepage Aggregation: Aggregate content from the Gallery, Music, About, and Contact pages into a unified homepage.
- Gallery Display: Showcase Yung Chaf's visual art, including both original photos and AI-generated images.
- Music Integration: Embed music content from platforms like TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, and Spotify, directly into the Music page.
- Fan Sign-Up and Login: Allow fans to create accounts and log in to access platform features.
- Content Interaction: Enable fans to like, comment on, and share Yung Chaf's music and art.
- Referral System: Implement a feature that allows fans to refer friends and family to Yung Chaf's music platform.
- AI-Powered Personalized Music Recommendation Tool: A feature powered by AI that recommends new or existing music by Yung Chaf that is likely to resonate with a given fan, based on their listening habits and stated preferences.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant purple (#9400D3) to reflect Yung Chaf's artistry and creativity.
- Background color: Light lavender (#E6E6FA), a desaturated version of the primary, to ensure readability.
- Accent color: Electric blue (#7DF9FF), an analogous hue to purple, will add contrast and highlight interactive elements.
- Body text: 'PT Sans' sans-serif font.
- Headline text: 'Playfair', a high contrast sans-serif, will give the headlines a modern and elegant appearance. Use 'PT Sans' for body text if longer blocks of text appear.
- Use icons related to music, art, and social media platforms.
- Responsive design to ensure optimal viewing experience across devices.
